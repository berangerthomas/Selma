# European Robin (*Erithacus rubecula*)

## Overview
The European robin is one of the best-loved birds on the continent, instantly recognisable by its vivid **orange-red breast** contrasting with its olive-brown back. It is the national bird of the United Kingdom.

## Territorial behaviour
Highly territorial year-round. Both sexes defend territories by singing vigorously, responding aggressively to intruders. Uniquely among passerines, it continues to sing throughout winter.

## Song
Its melodic, somewhat wistful song is delivered at dawn, dusk, and even at night near streetlights.

## Diet
Insects, earthworms, small molluscs, berries and small fruits in autumn and winter. Often seen close to gardeners, ready to snatch unearthed worms.

## Migration
Northern populations move south in winter; in western Europe many individuals are year-round residents.

## Conservation status
**Least Concern** (IUCN). A common species, though sensitive to harsh winters and cold snaps.
