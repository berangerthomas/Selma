# Blue Whale (*Balaenoptera musculus*)

## Overview
The blue whale is the largest animal ever known to have existed on Earth. It can reach **33 metres** in length and weigh up to **180 tonnes**.

## Feeding
Despite its colossal size, it feeds exclusively on **krill** (small crustaceans), filtering seawater through its baleen plates. It can consume up to **4 tonnes of krill per day**.

## Vocalisations
Produces the loudest sounds in the animal kingdom (up to 188 dB) — low-frequency infrasound audible thousands of kilometres away.

## Migration
Undertakes long seasonal migrations between polar feeding grounds (summer) and tropical breeding areas (winter).

## Conservation status
**Endangered** (IUCN). Intensive commercial whaling in the 20th century reduced the global population from ~350,000 to fewer than 25,000 individuals. The population has been recovering slowly since the international ban in 1966.
